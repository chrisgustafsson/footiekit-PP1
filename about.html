About FOOTIEKITS


We are football fanatics that finds a great interest in collecting football shirts and making them obtainable for every football fan. It all started as a hobby fueled by passion and ended up in a store all about football and football shirts.


Today we are located in Stockholm, Sweden providing football fans with an opportunity to find rare and exciting football shirts every weekday. We search far and wide for the best deals of football shirts and ship in new articles every week from all across the globe.
